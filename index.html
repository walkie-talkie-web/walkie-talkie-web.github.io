Connect Across Servers

Easily communicate across multiple Discord servers using frequency-based channels. Tune your server to a specific frequency, send messages, and receive them from other servers on the same frequency.

Frequency-Based Messaging

Send and receive messages on specific frequencies, connecting your server with others based on your chosen frequency. Only one frequency can be active at a time, ensuring focused communication.

Seamless Integration

Easily integrate the bot into your server. With simple slash commands, setup takes just seconds. Start sending messages across servers instantly.

Real-Time Communication

Experience fast and reliable real-time messaging across servers. All messages are delivered instantly to the channel designated for the chosen frequency.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Add the Bot to Your Server

1. Click the "Invite Bot" button (provide the bot invite link here).
2. Choose the Discord server you want to add the bot to.
3. Confirm the required permissions and click "Authorize."


Step 2: Select a Frequency

Once the bot is in your server, open any text channel.
1. Type /walkie-frequency and hit Enter.
2. Choose a frequency for your server by clicking one of the buttons that appear.
3. The bot will show you the active frequency in the channel.


Step 3: Send Messages

1. To send a message through the selected frequency, simply type your message in the channel.
2. The message will be transmitted to all other servers tuned to the same frequency.
